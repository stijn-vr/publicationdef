42mm Graphite Mars Lumograph 8B

Series of drawings 202 x 142 cm 2014

Previous Next
‘Rotating a supremely dark pencil, while pressing it hard into the paper’ is the formula of this work. These so called formulas are common used strategies in my work. Due to the rotation, the pencil shifts very slowly over the paper cau- sing a very intense and grainy line. It is very important that there is no intention nor goal to where the line goes, the drawer follows the pencil and not the other way around. The paper is just a part of a work that, in theory, exists infinitely. This might convey very conceptual, but it is only through the visual properties of this drawing -if we can call it that way- came into being and the experience provoked by the work is still essential.
